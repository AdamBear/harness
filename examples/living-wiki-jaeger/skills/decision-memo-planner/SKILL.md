# Decision Memo Planner

Turn a proposal into evaluation criteria, evidence needs, assumptions, options,
tradeoffs, and retrieval tasks. Keep the plan grounded in wiki pages, uploaded
sources, and existing artifacts.

Prefer explicit decision options: adopt, pilot, defer, or reject.

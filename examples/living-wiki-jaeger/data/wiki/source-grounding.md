# Source Grounding

Wiki pages should distinguish sourced claims from open questions. Curated edits
should preserve source references instead of replacing them with unsupported
summary text.

## Practices

- Cite source slugs in a `Source References` section.
- Keep unresolved items in `Open Questions`.
- Prefer compact updates over wholesale rewrites.
